    The city of Windsor’s Open Data Portal is an open source delivery tool to bring people and data together. Whether you’re an app developer, an engaged citizen, or a student trying to learn more about how the city works, the Open Data Portal contains a wealth of datasets for you to explore. We update regularly with new datasets, and welcome your requests.
